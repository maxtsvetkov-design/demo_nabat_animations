Ether

Nabat's self-supervised geospatial AI foundation model - trained on petabytes of coastal and arid habitat data at mm-level resolution, across millions of hectares.

It drives every analysis in NabatOS: from land cover classification and site suitability scoring to biomass estimation, carbon sequestration modeling, and real-time health change detection. Purpose-built for the ecosystems where no off-the-shelf model works.

NabatOS pipeline

NabatOS manages ecosystems from data capture to verified outcome.

Step 01

Data Capture

Multi-source ingestion from satellites, drone swarms, and on-ground IoT sensors for high-fidelity baselines.

Step 02

Assessment

AI-driven analysis to identify ecosystem health, biodiversity metrics, and carbon sequestration potential.

Step 03

Planning

Generative ecological modeling to design optimal restoration pathways and site-specific interventions.

Step 04

Restoration

Execution through automated dispersal and localized reforestation management systems.

Step 05

Monitoring

Real-time surveillance of growth metrics, water retention, and returning wildlife populations.

Step 06

Verification

Transparent, third-party auditable ledgers of ecological gains and carbon credits.
